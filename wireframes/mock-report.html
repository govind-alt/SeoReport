Recommendations & Next Steps
Action Plan — June 2024
📌 Priority legend: 🔴 Critical — fix immediately (blocks rankings/crawlability) · 🟡 High — complete by mid-month · 🔵 Medium — complete this month
1
🔴 Fix 3 Broken Internal Links
Three internal links point to deleted/moved pages: /blog/post-14, /resources/guide-3, and /old-services. Broken links harm crawlability, user experience, and pass negative signals to search engine bots. Fix by updating the URLs or adding 301 redirects.
🔴 Impact: Critical · Effort: Low · Timeline: Immediate (1 week)
2
🔴 Add Meta Descriptions to 8 Blog Pages
8 blog posts under the /blog/* subdirectory are currently missing meta descriptions. This forces search engines to generate arbitrary snippet summaries, hurting organic CTR. Create targeted, 150-160 character descriptions highlighting user value.
🔴 Impact: High · Effort: Low · Timeline: Within 2 weeks
3
🟡 Target Keyword Gap: 'ecommerce seo uk' (Vol: 720, Diff: 69)
Competitor rival-seo.co.uk ranks #5 for 'ecommerce seo uk', while you currently do not rank in the top 100. Create a dedicated landing page or detailed blog guide optimization cluster targeting this term. Include case study proof blocks to drive traffic and commercial conversion.
🟡 Impact: High · Effort: Medium · Timeline: 3–4 weeks
4
🟡 Optimise 11 Slow Pages (>3s Load Time)
11 templates and blog pages are currently exceeding safe 3-second load thresholds, posing Core Web Vitals LCP penalties. Compress heavy background assets into WebP format, activate lazy loading on images, and check server caching rules.
🟡 Impact: Medium-High · Effort: Medium · Timeline: Within this month
5
🔵 Build 5 Links from DA 60+ Domains
Acquire high-quality referring domain trust signals. Use targeted digital PR outreach and resource page pitching to industry authorities. Adding 5 high-authority backlinks will boost current Domain Trust toward 45+.
🔵 Impact: Medium-High · Effort: High · Timeline: Ongoing (6 weeks)
